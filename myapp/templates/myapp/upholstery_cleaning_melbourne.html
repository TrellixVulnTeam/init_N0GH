Upholstery Cleaning Melbourne

Upholstery Cleaning Melbourne

Sofas, armchairs, recliners and other types of upholstered furniture drive the greatest attention and serve as the favourite relaxation spot in a household. With everyday use, the upholsteries can absorb dust, dirt, dander, pollen, stains and other allergens which would pose serious health issues to people. Regular dusting and brushing techniques will not help eliminating these health culprits. So, hiring a professional upholstery cleaning service is highly recommended. If you live anywhere in Melbourne, you can count on Drymaster Carpet Cleaning for your upholstery cleaning needs.

Specialised Techniques:

We specialise in the latest techniques of upholstery cleaning and offer long lasting services to both residential and commercial clients. Our cleaning experts have sound knowledge on a wide range of cleaning methods and provide immense care that your upholsteries deserve.

Organic Cleaning Agents:

Using harsh cleaning solution will affect the surface of the upholstery and ruin its original texture. That’s why, we only use organic cleaning agents that are harsh on stains and dirt but mild on the upholstery fabric. Our organic cleaning solutions are safe for your kids, pets and the eco-system too.

Eliminate Infestation:

The surface of the upholstery is highly susceptible to the dust mite infestation which can be eliminated only through professional cleaning methods. At Drymaster Carpet Cleaning, we follow a comprehensive, step-by-step approach that involves soil removal, shampooing and steam cleaning to provide outstanding results.

Alleviate Pet Odour:

Our steam cleaning technique does not only eliminate the dust and dirt, but also alleviate pet odour completely. We use the right kind of cleaning equipment and tools to clean the linen couch, wool couch, micro suede and micro fiber surfaces.

So, if you would like to restore the beauty of your upholstered furniture and remove harmful allergens, call 03 9923 2799 for a no-obligation estimate today. Let us do whatever it takes to fulfill your upholstery & carpet cleaning needs.
