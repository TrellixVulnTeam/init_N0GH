Not All Services Can Be Booked Online.

The following services cannot be booked online: Items beyond the scope of normal cleaning processes such as trashed furnishings and carpets which may require industrial cleaning. Water Damage. Factory Rug cleaning. Pet and urine treatments. Colour spot dyeing. Repairs and speciality jobs. Real Estate and Body Corporation work.

Promotion code is DRY10

Don't forget to add the promotion code "DRY10" and click the APPLY button when you enter your information at the bottom of this page to receive your 10% discount.

Payment

We do not take payment online. You can pay the technician on site by cash / Visa or Mastercard on completion of job. A tax invoice receipt is issued on all jobs. To receive your 10% discount, payment must be paid on completion of job.

We will confirm your booking

We try our best to accommodate your booking on the day you requested and we will call you ASAP if there is a problem. Drymaster does not cover all suburbs outside metropolitan areas. We will call you if you are outside our coverage area. We normally respond to your booking request within a couple of hours or the following day. Your booking is not confirmed until we contact you.

Same day and next day bookings.

If you have a same day or next day booking please call us on 1300 662 188 and we will book you in over the phone and will still apply the 10% discount.
